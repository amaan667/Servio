# ADR 0045: Pricing and Revenue Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a clear pricing and revenue strategy to drive growth and profitability. Requirements include:
- Competitive pricing
- Tier-based plans
- Revenue optimization
- Pricing flexibility
- Value demonstration

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive pricing and revenue strategy with tier-based plans and value-based pricing. This provides:
- Competitive pricing
- Multiple tiers
- Revenue optimization
- Pricing flexibility
- Value demonstration

### Implementation Details

1. **Pricing Tiers**
   - Free tier (limited)
   - Starter tier (small venues)
   - Professional tier (medium venues)
   - Enterprise tier (large venues)
   - Custom pricing (special cases)

2. **Pricing Model**
   - Monthly subscriptions
   - Annual discounts
   - Per-location pricing
   - Usage-based add-ons
   - Enterprise custom pricing

3. **Revenue Optimization**
   - Upselling opportunities
   - Cross-selling
   - Expansion revenue
   - Add-on services
   - Tier upgrades

4. **Pricing Flexibility**
   - Trial periods
   - Money-back guarantee
   - Pause/resume
   - Downgrade options
   - Custom contracts

5. **Value Demonstration**
   - ROI calculators
   - Case studies
   - Success stories
   - Feature comparison
   - Value metrics

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Competitive pricing
  - Multiple tiers
  - Revenue optimization
  - Pricing flexibility
  - Value demonstration
- Negative:
  - Pricing complexity
  - Support overhead
  - Revenue volatility
  - Competitive pressure

## Alternatives Considered
- **Single tier**: Limited market, no flexibility
- **Freemium only**: Low conversion, limited revenue
- **Enterprise only**: Small market, high CAC
- **Usage-based only**: Unpredictable revenue, customer concern
