# ADR 0048: Risk Management Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a comprehensive risk management strategy to identify, assess, and mitigate risks. Requirements include:
- Risk identification
- Risk assessment
- Mitigation strategies
- Monitoring and review
- Business continuity

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive risk management strategy with regular reviews and mitigation plans. This provides:
- Risk identification
- Risk assessment
- Mitigation strategies
- Monitoring and review
- Business continuity

### Implementation Details

1. **Risk Identification**
   - Technology risks
   - Security risks
   - Operational risks
   - Financial risks
   - Legal/compliance risks

2. **Risk Assessment**
   - Probability assessment
   - Impact analysis
   - Risk scoring
   - Prioritization
   - Documentation

3. **Mitigation Strategies**
   - Prevention measures
   - Contingency plans
   - Insurance coverage
   - Diversification
   - Process improvements

4. **Monitoring and Review**
   - Regular risk reviews
   - Key risk indicators
   - Early warning systems
   - Incident analysis
   - Continuous improvement

5. **Business Continuity**
   - Disaster recovery
   - Backup strategies
   - Alternative providers
   - Communication plans
   - Recovery procedures

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Risk identification
  - Risk assessment
  - Mitigation strategies
  - Monitoring and review
  - Business continuity
- Negative:
  - Assessment overhead
  - Mitigation costs
  - Complexity
  - Resource allocation

## Alternatives Considered
- **No risk management**: Unprepared, reactive
- **Ad-hoc only**: Inconsistent, missed risks
- **Over-cautious**: Missed opportunities, slow growth
- **Minimal only**: Insufficient coverage
