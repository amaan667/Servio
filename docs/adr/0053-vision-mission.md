# ADR 0053: Vision and Mission

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a clear vision and mission to guide strategic decisions and inspire stakeholders. Requirements include:
- Clear vision statement
- Mission statement
- Core values
- Strategic objectives
- Long-term goals

## Decision
We will establish a comprehensive vision and mission with clear values and strategic objectives. This provides:
- Clear direction
- Strategic alignment
- Stakeholder inspiration
- Decision framework
- Long-term focus

### Implementation Details

1. **Vision Statement**
   - Future state
   - Market position
   - Customer impact
   - Industry leadership
   - Innovation focus

2. **Mission Statement**
   - Purpose
   - Value proposition
   - Target customers
   - Differentiation
   - Impact goals

3. **Core Values**
   - Customer focus
   - Innovation
   - Integrity
   - Collaboration
   - Excellence

4. **Strategic Objectives**
   - Short-term goals (1 year)
   - Medium-term goals (3 years)
   - Long-term goals (5+ years)
   - Key performance indicators
   - Success metrics

5. **Long-term Goals**
   - Market leadership
   - Customer satisfaction
   - Innovation leadership
   - Sustainable growth
   - Social impact

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Clear direction
  - Strategic alignment
  - Stakeholder inspiration
  - Decision framework
  - Long-term focus
- Negative:
  - Rigidity
  - Changing markets
  - Execution challenges
  - Communication overhead

## Alternatives Considered
- **No vision**: Lack of direction, poor alignment
- **Profit-only**: Short-term focus, poor culture
- **Growth-only**: Unsustainable, poor quality
- **Over-ambitious**: Unrealistic, demotivating
