# ADR 0039: Branding and Marketing Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a clear branding and marketing strategy to build awareness and acquire customers. Requirements include:
- Brand identity
- Marketing channels
- Content strategy
- Lead generation
- Customer acquisition

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive branding and marketing strategy with multiple channels and data-driven decisions. This provides:
- Strong brand identity
- Multiple marketing channels
- Content strategy
- Lead generation
- Customer acquisition

### Implementation Details

1. **Brand Identity**
   - Brand guidelines
   - Visual identity
   - Tone of voice
   - Value proposition
   - Differentiation

2. **Marketing Channels**
   - Content marketing (blog, guides)
   - SEO optimization
   - Social media
   - Email marketing
   - Paid advertising

3. **Content Strategy**
   - Blog posts
   - Case studies
   - Video content
   - Webinars
   - Whitepapers

4. **Lead Generation**
   - Landing pages
   - Free tools/resources
   - Webinars
   - Content upgrades
   - Referral program

5. **Analytics and Optimization**
   - Marketing analytics
   - Conversion tracking
   - A/B testing
   - ROI measurement
   - Continuous optimization

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Strong brand
  - Customer acquisition
  - Market awareness
  - Competitive advantage
  - Growth
- Negative:
  - Marketing costs
  - Resource requirements
  - Time investment
  - Complexity

## Alternatives Considered
- **No marketing**: No growth, invisible
- **Word of mouth only**: Slow, unreliable
- **Paid only**: Expensive, not sustainable
- **Content only**: Slow results
