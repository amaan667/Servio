# ADR 0038: Legal and Compliance Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs to comply with various legal and regulatory requirements. Requirements include:
- GDPR compliance
- PCI DSS compliance
- Terms of service
- Privacy policy
- Cookie policy

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive legal and compliance strategy with regular reviews and updates. This provides:
- Legal compliance
- User trust
- Risk mitigation
- Clear policies
- Regular updates

### Implementation Details

1. **Compliance Framework**
   - GDPR compliance
   - PCI DSS compliance
   - Data protection laws
   - Industry regulations
   - Regular audits

2. **Legal Documents**
   - Terms of service
   - Privacy policy
   - Cookie policy
   - Acceptable use policy
   - SLA agreements

3. **Data Protection**
   - Data minimization
   - Consent management
   - Data retention policies
   - Data deletion procedures
   - Data portability

4. **Compliance Monitoring**
   - Regular compliance reviews
   - Legal consultations
   - Industry updates
   - Policy updates
   - Training programs

5. **Incident Response**
   - Breach notification procedures
   - Legal notification requirements
   - User communication
   - Remediation procedures
   - Documentation

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Legal compliance
  - User trust
  - Risk mitigation
  - Clear policies
  - Competitive advantage
- Negative:
  - Legal costs
  - Complexity
  - Restrictions
  - Documentation overhead

## Alternatives Considered
- **Minimal compliance**: Legal risk, user distrust
- **No compliance**: Illegal, impossible
- **Over-compliance**: Unnecessary restrictions
- **Reactive only**: Too late, penalties
