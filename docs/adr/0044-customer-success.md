# ADR 0044: Customer Success Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a customer success strategy to ensure customer satisfaction and retention. Requirements include:
- Onboarding process
- Customer education
- Proactive support
- Success metrics
- Retention strategies

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive customer success strategy with proactive engagement and measurable outcomes. This provides:
- Better onboarding
- Customer education
- Proactive support
- Success metrics
- Higher retention

### Implementation Details

1. **Onboarding**
   - Structured onboarding flow
   - Welcome emails
   - Tutorial videos
   - Setup wizards
   - Success milestones

2. **Customer Education**
   - Knowledge base
   - Webinars
   - Best practices
   - Case studies
   - Training materials

3. **Proactive Support**
   - Health checks
   - Usage monitoring
   - Proactive outreach
   - Success managers (enterprise)
   - Regular check-ins

4. **Success Metrics**
   - Adoption rates
   - Feature usage
   - Satisfaction scores
   - Retention rates
   - Expansion revenue

5. **Retention Strategies**
   - Engagement programs
   - Value demonstration
   - Feedback loops
   - Success stories
   - Loyalty programs

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Better onboarding
  - Customer education
  - Proactive support
  - Success metrics
  - Higher retention
- Negative:
  - Resource requirements
  - Cost of success team
  - Measurement complexity
  - Time investment

## Alternatives Considered
- **Reactive only**: Poor retention, late intervention
- **Self-service only**: Low engagement, poor outcomes
- **No onboarding**: Poor first impression, churn
- **Minimal support**: Poor satisfaction
