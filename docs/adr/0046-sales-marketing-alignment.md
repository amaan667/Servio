# ADR 0046: Sales and Marketing Alignment

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs alignment between sales and marketing to ensure efficient customer acquisition and revenue growth. Requirements include:
- Lead handoff
- Messaging consistency
- Feedback loops
- Revenue attribution
- Collaborative planning

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive sales and marketing alignment strategy with clear processes and collaboration. This provides:
- Efficient lead handoff
- Consistent messaging
- Feedback loops
- Revenue attribution
- Collaborative planning

### Implementation Details

1. **Lead Handoff**
   - Lead scoring
   - Automated routing
   - Lead qualification
   - Handoff documentation
   - Follow-up processes

2. **Messaging Consistency**
   - Brand guidelines
   - Value proposition
   - Competitive positioning
   - Content alignment
   - Training materials

3. **Feedback Loops**
   - Sales feedback to marketing
   - Marketing feedback to sales
   - Customer feedback integration
   - Regular meetings
   - Shared metrics

4. **Revenue Attribution**
   - Lead source tracking
   - Campaign attribution
   - Conversion tracking
   - ROI measurement
   - Performance reporting

5. **Collaborative Planning**
   - Joint planning sessions
   - Campaign alignment
   - Product roadmap input
   - Market intelligence sharing
   - Goal alignment

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Efficient lead handoff
  - Consistent messaging
  - Feedback loops
  - Revenue attribution
  - Collaborative planning
- Negative:
  - Coordination overhead
  - Process complexity
  - Communication challenges
  - Resource allocation

## Alternatives Considered
- **Silos**: Poor alignment, inefficiency
- **Sales-led only**: Marketing disconnected, poor messaging
- **Marketing-led only**: Sales disconnected, poor conversion
- **No alignment**: Confusion, poor customer experience
