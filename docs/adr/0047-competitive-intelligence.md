# ADR 0047: Competitive Intelligence Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a competitive intelligence strategy to stay ahead of competitors and identify opportunities. Requirements include:
- Competitor monitoring
- Market analysis
- Feature comparison
- Pricing intelligence
- Opportunity identification

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive competitive intelligence strategy with regular monitoring and analysis. This provides:
- Competitor awareness
- Market insights
- Feature differentiation
- Pricing intelligence
- Opportunity identification

### Implementation Details

1. **Competitor Monitoring**
   - Feature tracking
   - Pricing monitoring
   - Marketing analysis
   - Product launches
   - Customer reviews

2. **Market Analysis**
   - Market trends
   - Customer needs
   - Technology shifts
   - Regulatory changes
   - Industry reports

3. **Feature Comparison**
   - Feature matrix
   - Differentiation analysis
   - Gap identification
   - Innovation opportunities
   - User feedback comparison

4. **Pricing Intelligence**
   - Price comparison
   - Discount tracking
   - Value analysis
   - Pricing trends
   - Competitive positioning

5. **Opportunity Identification**
   - Market gaps
   - Unmet needs
   - Emerging trends
   - Competitive weaknesses
   - Strategic opportunities

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Competitor awareness
  - Market insights
  - Feature differentiation
  - Pricing intelligence
  - Opportunity identification
- Negative:
  - Monitoring overhead
  - Analysis time
  - Resource allocation
  - Potential distraction

## Alternatives Considered
- **No intelligence**: Blind to competition, missed opportunities
- **Ad-hoc only**: Inconsistent, reactive
- **Over-focus**: Distraction from execution
- **Manual only**: Time-consuming, error-prone
