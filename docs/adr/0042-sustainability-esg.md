# ADR 0042: Sustainability and ESG Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a sustainability and ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) strategy to align with modern business practices. Requirements include:
- Environmental impact
- Social responsibility
- Governance practices
- Reporting and transparency
- Continuous improvement

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive sustainability and ESG strategy with measurable goals and regular reporting. This provides:
- Environmental responsibility
- Social impact
- Good governance
- Transparency
- Competitive advantage

### Implementation Details

1. **Environmental Impact**
   - Green hosting providers
   - Energy efficiency
   - Carbon footprint tracking
   - Remote-first culture
   - Digital optimization

2. **Social Responsibility**
   - Diversity and inclusion
   - Employee wellbeing
   - Community engagement
   - Ethical sourcing
   - Accessibility

3. **Governance Practices**
   - Ethical business practices
   - Data privacy
   - Security
   - Compliance
   - Transparency

4. **Reporting**
   - ESG metrics
   - Annual reports
   - Goal tracking
   - Third-party verification
   - Public disclosure

5. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Regular reviews
   - Goal setting
   - Stakeholder engagement
   - Best practices
   - Innovation

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Environmental responsibility
  - Social impact
  - Good governance
  - Transparency
  - Competitive advantage
- Negative:
  - Measurement overhead
  - Reporting costs
  - Implementation complexity
  - Trade-offs

## Alternatives Considered
- **No ESG strategy**: Missed opportunities, reputational risk
- **Minimal compliance**: Basic requirements only
- **Greenwashing**: Risk of backlash
- **Over-commitment**: Unrealistic goals
