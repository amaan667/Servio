# ADR 0034: Customer Support Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The Servio platform needs a comprehensive customer support strategy to ensure user satisfaction. Requirements include:
- Support channels
- Self-service options
- Ticket management
- Knowledge base
- Feedback collection

## Decision
We will implement a comprehensive customer support strategy with multiple channels and self-service options. This provides:
- Multiple support channels
- Self-service options
- Efficient ticket management
- Comprehensive knowledge base
- Continuous improvement

### Implementation Details

1. **Support Channels**
   - Email support
   - In-app chat (future)
   - Phone support (enterprise)
   - Community forum (future)
   - Social media (future)

2. **Self-Service**
   - Comprehensive documentation
   - FAQ section
   - Video tutorials (future)
   - Troubleshooting guides
   - Search functionality

3. **Ticket Management**
   - Ticket creation
   - Priority levels
   - SLA commitments
   - Escalation procedures
   - Resolution tracking

4. **Knowledge Base**
   - Articles and guides
   - Video tutorials
   - Best practices
   - Troubleshooting
   - Regular updates

5. **Feedback Collection**
   - In-app feedback
   - Post-support surveys
   - NPS tracking
   - Feature requests
   - Continuous improvement

## Consequences
- Positive:
  - Better user satisfaction
  - Reduced support burden
  - Self-service options
  - Continuous improvement
  - Competitive advantage
- Negative:
  - Additional costs
  - Resource requirements
  - Maintenance overhead
  - Complexity

## Alternatives Considered
- **Email only**: Limited channels, poor experience
- **No self-service**: High support burden
- **Community only**: Unreliable for business
- **No support**: Poor user experience
